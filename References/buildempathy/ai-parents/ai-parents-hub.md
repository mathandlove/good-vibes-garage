# AI + Parenting Hub

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/ai-parents

---

## Overview

Elliott created this platform to help parents explore thoughtful approaches to introducing children to artificial intelligence. Functions as a collaborative space for parenting in the AI era.

---

## Key Sections

**Prompt Library**  
Growing collection where parents can discover and share AI prompts designed for children. Community submissions encouraged.

**Community Engagement**  
Elliott actively connects with parents through:
- One-on-one virtual consultations via Calendly
- Emerging newsletter and occasional online meetups

## Notable Anecdote

"It's been 10 days since this girl texted me back. I don't know what to do?"

## "How to Learn (Almost) Anything" Program

Currently in prototype phase — one-on-one coaching targeting students:
- Teach ChatGPT utilization for learning and creation
- Foster curiosity, personal agency, and self-assurance
- Cultivate lifelong learning competencies

Actively recruiting pilot families for development feedback.
