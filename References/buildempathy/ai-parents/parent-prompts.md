# Parenting with AI Prompt Library

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/parent-prompts

---

## Overview

Community-sourced collection of prompts parents use with AI to support their children's growth and development. Parents can also add their own prompts.

---

## Featured Prompts

### Academic Support

**1. Socratic Discussion on Papers**  
Encourages children to think deeply by having AI "ask me thoughtful, open-ended questions that challenge my assumptions."

**2. Guided Paper Editing**  
Focuses on self-revision where AI points out unclear areas "without being judgmental" rather than rewriting the paper.

### Personal Development

**3. Athletic Goal-Setting**  
Helps young athletes explore "what's driving those goals, what obstacles I might face."

**4. Relationship Navigation**  
Guides teens to understand perspectives differently through questions "that help me explore how the other person might be feeling."

### Practical Life Skills

**5. Birthday Party Planning**  
Assists parents with decisions on "theme, location, number of guests, activities, food, and budget."

**6. Room Organization**  
Uses uploaded photos to help children create "a simple, step-by-step plan" for tidying their space.
