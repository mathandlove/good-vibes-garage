## Page Summary

mPath (Boulder-based) was recruiting parents for a study examining a new reading app for children ages 2-10.

- **Duration:** One-hour sessions
- **Locations:** Lafayette, Colorado lab; community centers; or participants' homes
- **Compensation:** $30 Amazon Gift Card
- **Timeline:** Rolling admissions beginning April 9, 2024; ongoing approximately one year

**Research:** Specifically targeting parents new to literacy instruction.  
**Tools:** Optional eye-tracking glasses and MIT-developed foot-worn emotional sensor (measuring excitement levels).

**Lead Researcher:** Dr. Elliott Hedman, Ph.D. MIT Media Lab.
