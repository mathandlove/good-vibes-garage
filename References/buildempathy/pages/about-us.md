## Founder: Dr. Elliott Hedman

**Background & Credentials:**
- Ph.D. from MIT Media Lab
- Inventor of a sensor measuring children's subconscious emotional responses
- Former positions at IDEO and Google
- Founded mPath consulting company

**Key Accomplishments:**
Dr. Hedman "helps elevate the voices of all children" and created Wonder.io, a free reading application for students struggling with literacy.

**Personal Note:**  
He maintains a humorous aspiration to pursue stand-up comedy for elementary-aged audiences.

## Company: mPath

**Origin & Focus:**  
A spinoff from the MIT Media Lab, mPath operates as a design consultancy specializing in creating inclusive digital experiences for young learners across diverse needs.

**Notable Work:**  
The firm has partnered with the Boys and Girls organization to amplify narratives from "hundreds of struggling students" using digital educational platforms.

**Client Portfolio:**  
LEGO Group, McGraw Hill, Starbucks, Hasbro, and Best Buy.

**Core Innovation:**  
The company leverages proprietary emotional response measurement technology to enhance user experience design for children.
