# Designing Wonder.io — Boys & Girls Club Research

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/designingwonderstories  
**Client:** Boys & Girls Club / Wonder.io  
**Sector:** App Design, Education, Literacy, Reluctant Readers

---

## Representation

The app was developed collaboratively over five years with Boys & Girls Club students, predominantly serving struggling readers from diverse backgrounds. The design process involved asking how reading could become something students would personally identify with and actively want to engage with.

Dr. Hedman served as a literacy instructor at the club, testing different books and incorporating student feedback weekly. The finalized app was then distributed across Denver's after-school club network.

---

## Methodology

Research employed two key measurement tools:
- **Eye-tracking analysis** revealed that readers would skip text but return to questions multiple times (2-3 times per question)
- **Emotion sensors** demonstrated the significance of question-answering and positive reinforcement for student engagement

---

## Iteration

The development team has created "over 70 different versions" of Wonder.io with ongoing refinements. Future updates aim to incorporate students' preferences for increased visual elements, comic formats, and humorous content.

---

## Outcome

- Students using Wonder.io demonstrated a **three-grade-level reading improvement**
- Platform has achieved international adoption
- Endorsements from struggling readers
- Free trial available
