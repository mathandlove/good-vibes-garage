# AI Tutor — Level Up (Writing Feedback Tool)

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/levelup  
**Type:** Free Google Docs Add-on  
**Access:** Google Workspace Marketplace

---

## Overview

Level Up is a free Google Docs add-on that provides personalized, just-in-time writing feedback to students. The tool emphasizes student-driven reflection and critical thinking development.

---

## Core Features

- Free personalized writing feedback integrated directly into Google Docs
- Student-selected improvement areas for personalized, relevant feedback
- Reader-focused approach emphasizing communication impact
- Incremental feedback delivery to prevent overwhelm
- Step-by-step improvement guidance
- Real-time evaluation of student edits
- Progress tracking with levels and advancement indicators
- Customizable rubrics for teachers and students
- **No student writing storage** — feedback saves securely to Google Drive

---

## Seven Core Principles

1. **Student Driven** — Learners choose their improvement focus
2. **Audience First** — Feedback addresses reader experience
3. **Bite Sized** — One challenge presented at a time
4. **Scaffolded Support** — Clear, sequential guidance provided
5. **Immediate Feedback** — Real-time evaluation of changes
6. **Progression and Engagement** — Visible improvement tracking
7. **Customizable** — Adaptable rubrics for unique writing goals

---

## Teacher Benefits

The platform addresses three primary educator needs:
- Reduces time spent on ineffective feedback while delivering targeted guidance
- Develops student independence and critical thinking through active engagement
- Models appropriate generative AI use as a learning support tool

---

## Privacy & Access

- **100% free** for both teachers and students
- No student writing stored
- Available through Google Workspace Marketplace
