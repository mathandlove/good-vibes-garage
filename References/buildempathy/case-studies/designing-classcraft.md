# Onboarding Teachers in Classcraft

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/designingclasscraft  
**Client:** Classcraft  
**Objective:** Increase conversion of teachers who registered but never engaged with the free trial

---

## Content Sections

**Representation:**  
Focused on educators who registered for the platform but never engaged with the complimentary trial.

**Methodology:**  
Using Unity, the team developed a streamlined Classcraft prototype for classroom testing and recorded teachers' interactions with the system. This approach enabled natural observation of the obstacles educators encountered when introducing the technology to students.

**Iteration:**  
Three successive design versions were created, progressively improving user comprehension for teachers and adoption rates among students.

**Outcome:**  
Teachers gained access to an enhanced onboarding interface at Classcraft.com, allowing classroom setup without requiring complex account configurations or advanced behavioral management features.
