# Classcraft Teacher Onboarding — mPath

**Source URL:** https://www.buildempathy.com/designingclasscraft  
**Scraped:** April 2026

---

## Representation
Classcraft sought to increase website visitor conversion by targeting teachers who signed up but never used the free trial.

## Methodology
mPath partnered with Unity to build a simplified Classcraft version for classroom testing. The team videotaped teacher experiences to naturally observe challenges when introducing the tool to new classes.

## Iteration
The design team developed three distinct versions of the new Classcraft, progressively enhancing teacher comprehension and student adoption rates.

## Outcome
Classcraft launched an improved onboarding experience at Classcraft.com, allowing educators to begin classroom activities without complex account setup or advanced behavioral management tool configuration.
