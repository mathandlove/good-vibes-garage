## Vision

Good Vibes Garage is the personal platform, upcoming podcast, and body of work of **Dr. Elliott Hedman** — builder, teacher, researcher. The site exists at the intersection of authority and warmth: a brilliant professor who also runs a maker space.

**Core thesis:** When the barrier to building drops to zero, the question shifts from "can you code?" to "do you have something worth making?" The through-line across all of Elliott's work is the **democratization of creation** — AI and vibe coding are the latest and most powerful chapter of that story.

**Two audiences, one site:**
1. **Vibe-coding curious** (eyeballs) — developers, creatives, learners who want to feel like they found their person at the edge of this shift
2. **Corporate buyers** (money) — companies who need to upskill employees in AI-assisted creation and need authority, credibility, and a track record fast

---

## Design Language

- **Mood:** Creative & edgy, grounded and approachable. Not cold, not corporate, not startup-generic.
- **Background:** Light warm cream (#F2F1EC) — editorial, readable, approachable
- **Accent:** Olive green (#6B7A2A) with warm orange (#D45C1A) as secondary
- **Typography:** Fraunces (serif display — editorial, unconventional, confident) + Syne (sans-serif body — clean, geometric, maker culture)
- **Texture:** Grain overlay (SVG noise at low opacity) gives tactile warmth
- **Motion:** Staggered scroll reveals, alive hover states, subtle entrance animations — purposeful, never flashy
- **Layout:** Asymmetric where appropriate, generous negative space, designed not assembled
