# Writing Section Completeness — Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-04-18

## Problem

The GVG Writing section already has the right structure and tone. But it's incomplete relative to Elliott's full body of work on BuildEmpathy.com. For someone doing a credential check — "is this person legit?" — incompleteness signals curation for image. Completeness signals seriousness.

The goal is not to add marketing. It is to make the archive feel like an honest record of the work.

## What Already Exists (and Should Not Change)

- Four-tier structure: Featured / Selected Writing / In the Press / Academic Work
- Dimmed treatment for academic work (correct — signals depth without competing for attention)
- Existing press grid with: The 74, SXSWEdu, Patch, MIT News, Wired, InformationWeek, LiveScience, HuffPost, Core77
- Existing selected writing list (13 items)
- Existing academic section (9 items)

## Design Principles

- **No structural changes.** The four-tier layout is correct.
- **No logo emphasis.** The press grid lists outlets as text; the names speak for themselves.
- **Completeness over curation.** All legitimate work gets included; depth comes from having it all, not from highlighting the best.
- **Humble tone.** This follows the danah boyd / Bret Victor model: put the work out in full, let the archive do the credential work.

## Out of Scope

- Adding images or thumbnails to press items
- Restructuring the section by era or topic
- Adding a homepage logo strip
- Any new page routes
